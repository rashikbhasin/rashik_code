Crowe publicly endorsed Barack Obama, whom he called the light and the future", in the United States presidential election, 2012, and urged Americans to vote for him.[71] He also publicly endorsed Julia Gillard, whom he called "Leader through tough times", in the Australian Labor Party leadership spill, June 2013, although he is unable to vote in Australia as he is not an Australian citizen.[72] Gillard lost the Labor leadership to Kevin Rudd, who subsequently lost the Australian federal election, 2013.

Crowe says he follows New Zealand's rugby union team, the All Blacks, and Australia in any other sport.[73] Two of his cousins, Martin Crowe and Jeff Crowe, captained the Black Caps New Zealand international cricket team.
